About



Tooberz is an open-source project that aims to provide a platform for users to share their knowledge and experience with other users.
The way it works is that users create an account, take a short quiz, find their channel, and share their knowledge with other content creators.




What We Aim to Do


We aim to allow small content-creators to grow their audience and their content by learning from other content creators, getting feedback from them, and sharing videos with other content creators.




Why Us?


So why us? There are other softwares where you can message creators and learn from them, but we offer not just a messaging app, we also offer benefits specifically catered for content creators. Our quiz helps you find your niche and other creators in the same niche as you. We have algorithims that are specifically designed to help you, and only you. With all these benefits and more, why not chose us?
